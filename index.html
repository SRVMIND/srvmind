How we work

An approach that
respects what you already know.

We don't replace your expertise with a model. We build the connective tissue that carries it through every layer of the stack.

STEP 01 / DISCOVER

Capture the way your domain actually thinks.

Workflows, edge cases, vocabulary, the unwritten rules. We sit with the people who do the work.

STEP 02 / DESIGN

Architect a system that holds that knowledge.

Resources, MCP servers, evaluation suites, agent graphs, UI surfaces — chosen to fit the way you operate.

STEP 03 / SHIP

Wire it into the tools your team uses.

VS Code, GitHub, Slack, your dashboards. The experience meets engineers where they already are.

STEP 04 / EVOLVE

Keep it aligned as everything changes.

Continuous evaluation, prompt + workflow versioning, regression checks. Quality stays measurable.
